
# Excel Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project focuses on building interactive business reports and sales analytics dashboards using Microsoft Excel. The dashboards were designed to analyze product performance, country-wise sales trends, and division-level business insights through data visualization and reporting techniques.

The project demonstrates practical business intelligence reporting using Excel for data analysis, KPI tracking, and management reporting.

---

# Business Problem

Businesses generate large amounts of sales data, but raw spreadsheets alone do not provide meaningful insights for decision-making.

The objective of this project was to create interactive Excel reports capable of:
- Tracking product performance
- Analyzing country-wise sales trends
- Identifying top and bottom-performing products
- Generating division-level business reports
- Presenting data visually for management reporting
